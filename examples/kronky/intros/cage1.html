Introducing the Cage Pusher and the Cage:

->

To operate:

  1. Pick up the Cage Pusher
  2. Go to just outside the cage and manoever so you are pointing the pusher at the cage
  3. "Fire" the Pusher by pressing "X"
  4. The Cage, and all in/on it, will fly in that direction (rounded to half ordinals: 45 degrees) until it hits something or lands on a floor

Note, to line yourself up prior to "Firing" the cagse, it may be useful to use the "Strafe" keys ("A" and "D")

Keys:

XFire! (The Cage Pusher)
Also, to recap...
AStrafe left
DStrafe right
