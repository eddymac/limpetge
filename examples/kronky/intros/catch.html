When something is hurtling towards you, you can ignore it, get out the way, or catch it. To catch something...

  1. Place yourself where something will "land" just in front of you.
  2. As the object is flying towards you, catch it by pressing "E"
  3. If successful - OwzAtt!!!

Keys:

ECatch the thing (as well as Pick Up)
Also, to recap, following is useful...
AStrafe left
DStrafe right
