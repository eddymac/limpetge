To complete this task, you need to pick up the "flower" and take it to the "exit".

->

To move, press "W" to go forward, "S" to go back, "<" to turn left, ">" to turn right. Also "A" to strafe left, and "D" to strafe right.

To go up a floor, just walk into a "Lift Spring"

->

To pick up the flower (or any other object) go up to it and press "E".

A summary:
