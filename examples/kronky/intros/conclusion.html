Well done for making it this far (unless you "jumped" ahead). Just a couple more generic things before these introductions are complete.

First - you can "Save" a point by pressing "ENTER", and restore it by pressing "BACKSPACE". You can only "save" one point at a time, and it is volatile in that it goes if you leave the level. It is useful if you are about to attempt anything that may fail.

The other is that you can "Fast Forward" time by pressing "G". This will speed things up by about a factor of five, and is useful if you need to wait for anything.

Also, you can quit the level by pressing "ESCAPE" should you get trapped somewhere.

Enjoy the rest of the levels.

A summary of keys:

ENTERCreate save point (overwriting the last one)
BACKSPACERestore save point
ESCAPEQuit level
GFast forward time
