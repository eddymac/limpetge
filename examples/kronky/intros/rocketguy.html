To use a rocket:

  1. Pick it up in the normal way (going to it and pressing "E")
  2. Turn so you are facing the direction you want to go.
  3. "Fire" it by pressing "X". You will then start flying.
  4. You can "steer" by pressing "<" and ">".
  5. You will continue to fly until you collide into something, when you will stop.

WARNING:The flower is delicate, crashing into that with the rocket will smash it - also "crashing" the rockey nearby it the resulting shockwave will also destroy the flower.

It is also woth noting, when carrying something, if you press "Q" you will "drop" it where you are, or pressing "C" will throw it!

To summarise:

XFire rocket
<Steer left
>Steer right
Also...
QDrop or place an object.
CThrow it.
